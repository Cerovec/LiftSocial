Welcome to LiftSocial

LiftSocial enables you to customize your social networking sites easily. You can simply download a social network template, upload your own graphics, add htmls or texts, change the logo and arrange web pages.

LiftSocial is developed in Scala/Lift technology and even allows you to add 3rd party widgets, Google Adsense and other banner ads, Google Analytics, Flash, and videos. You can also customize the look and feel, change colors, font size, background, and other CSS elements.

With our social network solution, you can start your own version of Facebook and MySpace clone easily. The social networking template includes all CSS design elements for creating your own social networking site. Our implementation of comet technology inspires action and creates rich user experience.
